Servicios

System integration

Implementation, configuration and communication between industrial protocols, gateway and servers.


Nets: ProfiNet, Profibus DP, Profibus PA, Asi, ControlNet, DeviceNet, Data Highway Plus (DH+), Ethernet, Modbus TCP/IP, Modbus RTU, INTERBUS Phoenix Contact.

Industrial Automation

PLC - DCS - SAFETY programming (Siemens, Rockwell, Schneider, ABB, Honeywell, Delta, Invensys).

ROBOT programming (KUKA, ABB, FANUC, YASKAWA - MOTOMAN, KAWASAKI).

Design and implementation of SCADA - HMI systems (Siemens, Rockwell, Schneider, Honeywell, Invensys-Archestra, Wonderware InTouch, iFix).

Speed variators - servomotors (AB, SEW, ABB, SIEMENS, DANFOSS).

Pneumatics, Hydraulics, Migration and optimization of existing systems, Turnkey equipment development.

Fabrication and Assembly of Electrical Boards

Development of basic and detailed engineering of BT and MT boards, peripheral control system boards and field networks.


Distribution of Power Boards.

Domotics (home automation)

We bring technology into your home, in order to improve the quality of life of people.

Security systems, light control, automatic curtains, voice commands, electronic locks, etc.

Vision systems

Quality control, Disposal due to failures in the product.


Detection of coordinates for robotic systems. Process control.Traceability.

Commissioning and Start up

Strategy and implementation of a verification protocol for installations in energized condition, both by electric cause and in circumstances of fluids and pressures entry.


Verification of the appropriate conditions to start a production process.

Web Developments and APP

Developing web and mobile applications completely tailored to our clients and their needs.

Arduino Developments - PIC

Developing applications with Arduino, PIC, application electronic circuits, 3D printers, Routers, CNC.

Training Courses

We develop and provide courses in the area of industrial automation, fitting the needs of the client, PLC-SCADA-HMI programming, configuration of instruments and equipment, industrial networks.
